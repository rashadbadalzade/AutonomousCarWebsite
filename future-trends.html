FUTURE OF SELF-DRIVING AUTOMOBILES

The future of self-driving automobiles is poised for significant growth, driven by advancements in AI, sensor technology, and regulatory support. Key trends include: Increased Safety: Reduction in accidents caused by human error. Efficiency and Traffic Management: Optimized traffic flow and reduced congestion through intelligent routing. Environmental Benefits: Potential reduction in emissions through optimized driving patterns and increased adoption of electric vehicles. Accessibility: Enhanced mobility for the elderly and disabled.
